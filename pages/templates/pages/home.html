Miami Valley Packaging Solutions is focused on providing packaging solutions for our customers primarily through corrugated cartons, shipping and storing materials, corrugated boxes, die cut, partitions, and other specialty packaging. Our goal is to help our customers grow their business increase revenues, decrease costs, and operate more efficiently.


We have formed strategic partnerships which has expanded our knowledge and options to include, plastic corrugated, foam, and chipboard. With our expertise, we can solve any packaging problem in a creative, cost-competitive, efficient manner, we have been recognized in our indusrty with several design awards.


We Solve Problems By:


Package Design: The very foundation of our company is to be a solutions provider to our customers, that is why we made it part of our name. Our award-winning design department is critical in achieving customer solutions, through creating unique designs tailored to our customers needs, integrating different substrates as necessary including plastic corrugated and foam.


Innovation: Our team members are constantly looking at new materials and manufacturing processes to create more opportunities to be a solutions provider for our customers. Some of the innovative items we have developed include; Converting plastic corrugated for returnable packaging. Assembly of complete packaging including integrating foam and other materials. Creating unique delivery schedules to meet our customers needs.


Customer Service: By understanding our customers needs and industry, we become a solutions provider that integrated the unique facets of each company with the needs of the industry they serve. Our team coordinated efforts to get our customers what they want when they need it. From design to order entry to through production and into their hands to pack, our goal is their goal.
